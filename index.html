about me

A full time missionary serving at Campus Crusade for Christ under the Student Led Movement (SLM) Component.

My passion is that every individual in Eswatini and beyond would have the opportunity to hear the Gospel. One of the most strategic ways to help spread the Gospel around the world is by reaching University and Highschool Students, who will be the leaders of tomorrow and faculty who are influencers of today. Hence our motto in SLM “Win the Campus Today, Win the World Tomorrow”.

problem statement

The entire world today is filled with fear, anxiety and frustration. I always imagine how the world would have become if everyone knew Christ and they were discipled, especially those in authorities. However, a lot of students especially are without Christ and lack proper and effective discipleship, which has led to increased rate in suicide, drug abuse, depression, relationship dynamics and many more. A world without hope and peace.

personal vision

To expose students to the Gospel and effective discipleship, raising future leaders who know Christ through evangelism, discipleship (Follow-Up), educating and training them the foundational truths of Christianity.

roles in ministry

- Evangelism and discipleship (follow-up)

- Equipping students with Skills to achieve academic excellence through study with success program (on topics such as: goal setting, summery writing, taking notes, setting priorities, being proactive, personal sleep needs, daily performance curve etc.)

- Offering counselling to students with mental illnesses (i.e stress, depression, suicide ideation)

- Providing educational sessions related to mental health (i.e stress management, emotional intelligence etc.)

CHALLENGES IN MINISTRY

- Insufficient resources (for organizing trainings, camps etc.)

- Insufficient equipment and materials to do effective ministry (i.e projectors, laptops, cameras, printer, note pad)

- Transportation: Currently have one car in SLM which cannot transport all staff to different parts of Eswatini
